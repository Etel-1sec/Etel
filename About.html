About Etel

Revolutionizing urban transportation with sustainable, accessible e-mobility solutions.

Our Story

Etel was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to transform urban commuting through accessible, eco-friendly transportation. We recognized the growing challenges of urban mobility – congestion, pollution, and the high cost of transportation – and set out to create a solution that addresses all three.

Starting with e-rickshaws, we've developed a comprehensive platform that connects passengers directly to drivers of electric vehicles. Our journey begins with revolutionizing the rickshaw experience, but our ambition extends to creating an integrated network of all electric mobility options including e-scooters, e-bikes, and e-cars.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to make sustainable transportation the default choice for urban commuters. By combining cutting-edge technology with affordable electric vehicle options, we're building a future where clean mobility is accessible to everyone.

Etel isn't just a transportation service – it's a movement toward smarter, cleaner cities and a commitment to reducing the environmental impact of daily commutes while improving the livelihood of drivers in our communities.

Our Values

Sustainability

Every trip with Etel contributes to reducing carbon emissions. We're committed to measuring and improving our environmental impact continuously.

Accessibility

We believe sustainable transportation should be available to everyone, regardless of income level or location within the city.

Innovation

Our technology platform is constantly evolving to improve the experience for both riders and drivers, making e-mobility more efficient and enjoyable.

Community

We support our drivers with fair compensation and growth opportunities, creating positive economic impact in the communities we serve.

The Etel Difference

What sets Etel apart is our exclusive focus on electric vehicles. While other platforms may include some electric options, our specialized approach allows us to optimize the entire experience around the unique benefits and requirements of e-mobility.

Our platform is designed from the ground up to support electric vehicle drivers with features like charging station mapping, battery management insights, and customized routing to maximize efficiency and range.

For passengers, this means not just a ride, but a consistently clean, quiet, and modern transportation experience that they can feel good about choosing.

Join Our Journey

Etel is just beginning. As we launch our e-rickshaw service, we're already working on expanding to other electric vehicle types and new markets. Our vision is to create a comprehensive e-mobility network that makes sustainable transportation the most convenient choice for any urban journey.
